My Digital Product Journey

A personal story of starting LaxNar Studio and slowly figuring things out step by step.

The idea that kept coming back

I did not really have a perfect plan when I started this. I just knew I wanted to build something of my own online, something that felt meaningful to me, even if I did not fully understand how it would work yet. There was this idea that kept sitting in my mind for a long time. The idea of creating something online, building digital products, and having my own creative space kept coming back no matter how many times I ignored it.

Learning before creating

At first, I spent more time learning than actually doing anything. I watched, read, researched, and tried to understand how people even start digital products. The more I learned, the more confused I felt at times, but I also started realizing something important. Nobody really starts knowing everything. They just start and figure it out while moving.

The fear of not being ready

I used to feel like I needed more skills before I could begin. But at some point I realized that waiting to feel ready was just another way of not starting at all.

When it finally became real

Things started changing when I stopped treating this like something I would do later and started treating it like something I am already doing. Even small actions like creating a page, writing posts, or setting up ideas made everything feel real.

What I am learning now

Right now I am still learning everything. I am still figuring out my style, my products, and what I actually want to create long term. It is not fully clear yet, and that is okay because clarity is something I am building through action.

A lesson about small steps

One thing I did not understand before is how small progress actually looks. It is not always exciting. Most of the time it feels slow and invisible. But those small actions are the reason anything exists at all.

I stopped waiting for the perfect beginning and started building with what I had.

I don’t know exactly how everything is going to turn out for me, but I know I am not going to give up just because I feel behind sometimes, and maybe you don’t need to give up either, even if things feel unclear right now.

I don’t know who you are, but if you are reading this, I just want to say you can do it. I hope things get better for you, and I wish you good luck on your journey.
