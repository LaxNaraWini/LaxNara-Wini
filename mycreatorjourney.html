Finding My Flow: How I'm Starting to Get Leads on My Creator Journey

An honest reflection on building LaxNar Studio from scratch, celebrating the small wins, and learning how to connect with people who truly align with your work.

The quiet magic of the very first lead

When you start a digital product shop or a creative journal from zero, it can feel like you are shouting into an empty room. You spend hours designing templates, choosing colors, and writing from the heart, wondering if anyone will ever see it. But recently, something shifted for me. I started noticing the very first signs of traction—leads, inquiries, and small notifications showing that real people are starting to find LaxNar Studio. It made me realize that things are working, even when the progress feels slow and quiet.

Shifting from "chasing" to "attracting"

When I first started, I was constantly worried about how to get people to look at my shop. It felt heavy. But the moment I stopped treating my journey like a sales pitch and started sharing it as an honest, soft living journey, people began to naturally wander in. Getting leads isn’t about using pushy marketing tricks; it’s about putting your genuine energy out into the world and creating a space that feels like a warm hug. When your work comes from a place of alignment, the right people will find their way to you.

Small actions that helped open the doors

If you are also on a beginner creator path and waiting for your first few connections, I want to share a few small habits that quietly helped me change the momentum:

  • "Showing up authentically on Pinterest and socials."
  • "Focusing entirely on creating things that I personally love and would use on my own healing journey."
  • "Detaching from the numbers and celebrating every single person who stops by to read or follow."
  • "Trusting that the value I create will ripple out to the exact community that needs to see it."

Every view, save, or click is a real human being on the other side of the screen who resonates with the energy of LaxNar Studio. Remembering that turns a simple "metric" into a genuinely beautiful interaction.

You don't need a massive audience to make a meaningful impact. You just need to show up as yourself, and let the right souls gather.

If you're currently in that sticky, quiet phase where you're putting in the work but the results haven't fully blossomed yet, please don't give up. The leads will come, the shop visits will grow, and your community will expand. Trust the timing of your creative seeds—they are growing roots under the surface before they push through the soil. 🌸

With so much love, LaxNar Studio 💗
